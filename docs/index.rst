Why Login to Your Pogo Games Account?
=====================================

Logging in allows you to:

* Access your saved game progress
* Continue unfinished games
* View achievements and rewards
* Manage your profile
* Access Club Pogo membership benefits
* Connect with friends
* Participate in multiplayer games

How to Login to Your Pogo Games Account
=======================================

Follow these simple steps:

1. Open your preferred web browser.
2. Visit the official Pogo Games website.
3. Click the **Sign In** button.
4. Enter your registered email address.
5. Type your password.
6. Click **Sign In**.
7. Your Pogo dashboard will open after successful authentication.

Login on Mobile Devices
=======================

You can also sign in using your smartphone or tablet.

* Open your browser.
* Visit the Pogo website.
* Tap **Sign In**.
* Enter your login credentials.
* Tap **Login** to access your account.

Forgot Your Password?
=====================

If you cannot remember your password:

* Go to the Sign In page.
* Select **Forgot Password**.
* Enter your registered email.
* Follow the password reset instructions sent to your email.
* Create a new secure password.
* Return to the login page and sign in.

Common Login Problems
=====================

Incorrect Password
------------------

Double-check your password and ensure Caps Lock is turned off.

Forgot Email Address
--------------------

Use the email associated with your EA/Pogo account.

Browser Issues
--------------

Clear your browser cache and cookies, then try again.

Internet Connection
-------------------

Make sure your internet connection is stable before attempting to log in.

Account Locked
--------------

Multiple failed login attempts may temporarily lock your account. Wait a few minutes before trying again.

Tips for Secure Login
=====================

* Use a strong password.
* Never share your login credentials.
* Sign out after using public computers.
* Keep your browser updated.
* Avoid logging in on untrusted devices.
* Change your password regularly.
